Rescu Foundation

The Hunyani Rotary Club has embarked on a project to supply prosthetic limbs through Rescu.


Jaipur limbs are very effective low cost prosthetic legs, originally developed in India.

Working with the Ministry of Health and Child Welfare, Rescu is about to set up a centre to provide Jaipur Limbs for the whole of Zimbabwe.

This will complement the existing services for prosthetic limbs within the country.

The project is managed by technically trained and registered personnel, and support work is done by handicapped beneficiaries within Rescu.
